Lancer Cookie

Recipe by me!!!
If you want some for free just see me at the bakery if you're too lazy to make this recipe! I don't blame you for being such a couch potato though since this stuff takes some real tough cakes to do!

  • Time: 2 minutes
  • Serves: 1 darkner (or lightner)
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2 Spades
Sugar 6 Pails
egg
Pebbles 1 Handful
Sugar Cookies 1 Pack of 36

Step 1: Scoop 2 spades of flour into your pail and then pour in your 6 pails of sugar into that one pail

Step 2: Stir with your hand until it's powerdery and then dump in your pebbles and continue stiring

Step 3: Throw out everything you just did and open your pack of sugar cookies and line them up real neatly

Step 4: Bite into the cookies to try to sculpt a Lancer-shaped head out of it

Step 5: Use your finger to make a Lancer face with the leftover cream, and then you're done!

RouxlsRoux

By Rouxls Kaard
Thoust my favorite succulent supper whilst I was of a wee lad. here, I shalleth teacheth you thoust recipe so henceforth thou shalleth be ableth to recreate O favorited doted passtime of I! Aforementioned injurys or grief shall not be of mineth fault.

  • Time: 8 hours
  • Serves: 1 darkner
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 3/4 Cups
Butter 2 Tbsp
Dark Candy 3 Pieces

Step 1: Turneth on thou saucepan charging towards loweth possible capibilities and minceth dark candy forth advance

Step 2: Melteth all thou butter and addeth all thou flour to thou pan

Step 3: Wait 2 hours

Step 4: Burneth everything

Step 5: Generouxsly add choppeth dark candy and stireth wielding whisk forth a mere 6 hours

Step 6: Poureth thou roux into molds and storeth inside ice box for subsequent pastas and soups

Source: Deltarune
